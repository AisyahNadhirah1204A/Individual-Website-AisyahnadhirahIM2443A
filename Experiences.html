Internship UiTM Melaka Library

Internship or industry training usually as the starter for student to learn and adapt with new environment in work life in the future. As a student of library management in diploma, I registered to intern in Perpustakaan Tun Abdul Razak (PTAR) UiTM Lendu, Melaka. This internship is in March 2021, in about 6 weeks of practical. I learnt so many things during having my internship such as i learn on how to held a program, create a project and generated ideas. I also in charged in all departments in PTAR UiTM Melaka Library that have 6 departments. I got the best practical student during my internship because of my behaviour and tasks. This experience really gave me the big impact of me to be more responsible in future especially in work area.

Being part of Komander UiTM Kedah

I started to be part of "Komander Bulan Sabit Merah Malaysia (BSMM)" UiTM Kedah when i was in part 1, semester 1 during my diploma. It is because i want to try to become more active and at the same time I want to stay in hostel. I learnt various things about health because we as a komander must have the skills, and basic to help people such as injured people. We also held so many programs and exhibition about health to students of UiTM Kedah and I am an EXCO logistic which arranged the schedule of our programs such as drugs awareness and many more. I hold this position until in the last semester which is semester 4 and every semester, we will have the camping and it is as the test for our stamina.

My virtual exhibition is available at UiTM Melaka Library website and they put in part of virtual exhibition. Go take a look if you have free time.
